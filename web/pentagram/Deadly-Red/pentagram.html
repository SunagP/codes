⛧Deadly-Red ⛧

Rules

  • The game is played in 1vs1 format.
  • Each player on his turn can pick any number
    of coins(atleast 1) from any straight line
    on the star(colinear coins)
  • The player who will be left only
    with the red coloured coin
    in the middle to pick loses the game.
  • The player to start first is chosen by a
    coin toss.
  • Every move has a restricted time of 30 sec
