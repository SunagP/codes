Become the wealthiest player through buying, renting and selling of property and force other players into bankruptcy.
Each player's token is placed  on the corner marked "GO", and dice can be rolled to move his piece the number of spaces indicated by the dice.
Two or more tokens may rest on the same space at the same time.
Depending on the space the token reaches, you may buy the property, or be obliged to pay rent, pay taxes, draw a Chance or Community Chest card, Go To Jail, or etc...
Each time a player's token lands on or passes over GO, Rs 500 is credited to the player.
When you land on an unowned property you can buy that property from the Bank at its shown price.
When you land on a property that is owned by another player, the owner collects rent from you in accordance with the shown rent.
When you land on community chest or chance random tasks are shown and money is credited or debited in accordance with them.
When you land on income tax you pay a tax of Rs250
When you land on Jail you pay a penalty of Rs500
When you land on go to jail you are sent to jail and Rs500 is debited as penalty.
